Customer Support

The front line of user happiness and problem-solving.

A Team Leader reports to the OE Head (Operation Unit Head), who manages the entire project. The agents, in turn, report to the Team Leader.

Most people start as a support agent. After gaining experience, they can move to different projects or be promoted to roles like Team Leader.

The growth is very good. With good performance, the path is:

Path: Agent → Team Leader → Operation Head → Manager

A bachelor's degree (like B.Com) is a common qualification. However, years of experience in the customer support field are often just as important.

We coordinate with Reporting, Quality, and Operations teams. This involves sharing performance reports, handling escalations, and ensuring customer issues are solved on time.

The main tools are:

  • Outlook (for emails)
  • Excel (for reports)
  • CRM Tools (for tracking customer issues)
  • Internal company tools (for billing and data management)

We handle product-related and service issues, such as delivery delays, technical problems, or policy-related concerns. The goal is to give every customer a clear and polite solution.

People who are calm, polite, and good at communication. This job suits those who like helping others and can handle multiple tasks patiently.

By solving problems quickly and correctly, we keep customers happy. This builds trust and loyalty towards the company, which improves overall business growth.
