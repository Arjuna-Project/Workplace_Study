QA Engineer

← Back to All Roles
1. What do they do?
Find bugs, write test cases, run manual & automation tests
2. Who do they report to?
QA Lead, QA Manager, or Product Manager
3. Starting point
Manual Tester or via a software testing course
4. Career path
Paths to SDET, Architect, Lead, or Manager
5. Qualification
Skills in Java/Python & Selenium/Playwright are key
6. Interaction with teams
Works closely with Developers & in Scrum meetings
7. Tools used
Jira · Selenium · Playwright · Postman · Jenkins · Git
8. Problems solved
Changing requirements, tight deadlines, & tricky bugs
9. Who enjoys this job?
Curious 'guardians' with high attention to detail
10. Manual vs. Automation?
A balance of both. Automation for repetition, Manual for human insight
11. Role change with AI
AI enables smarter, 'shift-left' & predictive testing
